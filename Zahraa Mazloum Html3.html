KEEP YOUR MIND HEALTHY: MENTAL HEALTH IS VERY IMPORTANT

You should take care of yourself so that you may focus on the things that are important to you in life,
whether they are working, learning[1],
caring for your family, volunteering, enjoying the outdoors...

Having good mental health enables you to enjoy life and deal with challenges.
It provides a sense of security and indomitable spirit.x
In actuality, maintaining a healthy diet and exercise routine can support mental wellness.

Your emotions, physical health, and level of energy can all be directly impacted by the food you eat.
A "healthy diet" is one that has the recommended amounts of all the fundamental food categories,
a variety of foods from each, the proper number of calories to maintain a healthy weight, and is low in fat, sugar, salt..
You can maintain the health of your body by selecting foods that will provide you consistent energy. Additionally, it might make your mind feel fantastic. Not everyone responds well to the same diet. Speak with a health care expert to learn which foods are best for you.

Your mind and body will feel better if you sleep well.
Your body needs time every day to rest and heal.
If you often have trouble sleeping — either falling asleep, or waking during the night and being unable to get back to sleep —
one or several of the following ideas might be helpful to you:

  1. Go to bed at the same time every night and get up at the same time every morning.
  2. Avoid caffeine, nicotine, and alcohol
  3. Eat on a regular schedule and avoid a heavy meal prior to going to bed
  4. Exercise daily, but avoid strenuous or invigorating activity before going to bed
